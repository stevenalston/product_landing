The Future of Music

RatedNxt provides artists and fans with a musically centered fantasy experience. The RatedNxt story is fundamentally about transformation - of music discovery, product distribution talent acquisition, artist-fan interaction, and the reconstruction of revenue generation, and our ability to actively shape our futures in a notoriously challenging industry. However, we need your help! You can help support RatedNxt and these musicians by signing up to be an initial beta user with RatedNxt. Visit our IndieGoGo campaign to find out more about the app and how you can help.

Generate Revenue

Utilizing Rated Nxt’s immersive technology, budding musicians are able to gain & interact with fan base, compete with fellow artists for rankings, acquire essential tools for artist development, and generate revenue.

Data Analytics

Record labels now have a new arena to discover talent, vet strong/weak musicians and have access to in-depth analytics of the artists public perception within the platform.

Artist Tools

Users/Fans can discover new music, download media, interact with artists and through fantasy competition, can challenge friends via “critic contests.”
